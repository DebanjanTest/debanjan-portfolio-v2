## 🛠️ Project Showcases & Feature Breakdowns

### 1. AudioTune-AI
*   **Live App URL:** [audio-tune-ai.vercel.app](https://audio-tune-ai.vercel.app)
*   **Tech Stack:** Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Gemini AI
*   **Key Sections & Features:**
    *   **Audio Upload Zone:** Drag-and-drop or click upload surface supporting MP3, WAV, FLAC, M4A, AAC, OGG, MP4, and MOV formats.
    *   **AI Processing Panel:** Leverages Gemini AI to perform audio analysis, metadata extraction, noise reduction suggestions, and custom AI effects.
    *   **Audio Editor UI:** Visually renders audio waveforms and allows basic editing, trimming, and manipulation of audio tracks.
*   **Use Cases:** Quick client-side audio analysis, speech transcript summaries, and noise cleanup for content creators.

### 2. ATS Bridge (High-Fidelity Reconstructor)

*   **Key Sections & Features:**

*   **Use Cases:** Job seekers looking to optimize their resumes for corporate screening filters.

## 🎬 3D Deck Carousel Auto-Panning Preview
To show off the details of these projects directly within the portfolio's 3D cards, the active card's preview iframe is equipped with a smooth, continuous auto-panning CSS animation. 

*   **How it works:** The iframes are rendered at `200% width` and `200% height` (desktop view) and scaled down to `0.5` via CSS transform to fit their containers. A slow 28-second CSS animation shifts the `translate` offset of the iframe relative to the card wrapper, causing the live site's viewpoint to gently scan different regions of the application (scrolling down to display key features, and scrolling back up).
*   **Interaction:** Hovering over the active card pauses the panning animation (`animation-play-state: paused`), allowing the user to view a specific section.
